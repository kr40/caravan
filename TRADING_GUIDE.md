## Tips for Maximum Profit

1. **Focus on Luxury Goods**: Wine and Spices have the highest profit margins
2. **Plan Long Routes**: Multi-city loops maximize profit per trip
3. **Watch Your Cargo Space**: 100 units max (buy more carts later!)
4. **Balance Risk**: Longer routes = more profit but more bandit risk (future)
5. **Track Averages**: The game shows your average buy price vs current sell price
